# LegalPage AI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Positioning

LegalPage AI is a modern legal-tech SaaS brand for founders, makers, app developers, ecommerce operators, agencies, and AI startups.

The brand should feel:

- Premium
- Minimal
- Trustworthy
- Fast
- Technical but approachable
- Legal-tech, not traditional law firm

LegalPage AI should not feel overly corporate, intimidating, ornate, or academic. The visual language should communicate clarity, automation, reliability, and modern software quality.

## Logo Concept

The logo combines three ideas:

- A legal scale for trust and policy structure
- A document/page silhouette through the central vertical form
- A blue-to-purple AI gradient for technology and automation

The mark is intentionally simple so it works as:

- Favicon
- App icon
- Dashboard brand mark
- Social avatar
- Marketplace thumbnail
- White-label adaptable icon

## Color Palette

### Primary Blue

- Hex: `#2563EB`
- Use for primary actions, links, active states, and trust signals.

### Premium Purple

- Hex: `#7C3AED`
- Use for gradients, AI accents, featured badges, and upgrade moments.

### Deep Ink

- Hex: `#0B1026`
- Use for major headlines and high-emphasis text.

### Slate Text

- Hex: `#475569`
- Use for body copy and secondary descriptions.

### Soft Surface

- Hex: `#F8FAFC`
- Use for page backgrounds, dashboard bands, and subtle UI surfaces.

### Gradient

Use this as the primary brand gradient:

```css
linear-gradient(135deg, #2563EB 0%, #4F46E5 52%, #7C3AED 100%)
```

## Typography

Recommended font family:

```css
Inter, ui-sans-serif, system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif
```

Typography direction:

- Use strong, compact headlines.
- Keep body text calm and clear.
- Avoid decorative typefaces.
- Avoid overly playful lettering.
- Letter spacing should be neutral except for small uppercase labels.

## UI Style

LegalPage AI UI should use:

- White and slate surfaces
- Blue/purple gradient accents
- 10-16px radius for product cards and panels
- Small, purposeful shadows
- Clear borders
- High contrast text
- Minimal iconography
- Strong empty/loading/error states

Avoid:

- Heavy glassmorphism
- Neon palettes
- Beige legal-office styling
- Dense decorative backgrounds
- Overly rounded pill-heavy interfaces
- Cartoon legal illustrations

## Logo Usage

### Preferred Usage

Use the full logo on:

- Landing page header
- Marketplace screenshots
- Documentation covers
- Login pages

Use the icon mark on:

- Favicon
- App icon
- Sidebar collapsed states
- Mobile navigation
- Social avatars

### Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least half the icon width.

### Minimum Size

- Full logo: 160px wide minimum
- Icon mark: 24px wide minimum
- Favicon: 16px minimum

## Background Usage

The logo works best on:

- White
- Very light slate
- Deep ink
- Subtle blue/purple gradient backgrounds

For dark backgrounds, use the icon mark in its gradient container and white wordmark text if needed.

## Voice and Messaging

Brand voice should be:

- Confident
- Clear
- Practical
- Responsible
- Founder-friendly

Use phrases like:

- Generate legal pages in seconds
- AI-assisted legal page drafts
- Built for SaaS, apps, ecommerce, and AI tools
- Save, edit, export, and manage legal documents
- Always review with qualified legal counsel

Do not claim:

- Legally guaranteed
- Lawyer-approved
- Fully compliant
- Replaces legal counsel
- Certified legal documents

## Disclaimer Standard

Generated legal content must include:

```text
This document is AI-generated and does not constitute legal advice. You should review it with a qualified legal professional before publishing or relying on it.
```

## Marketplace Presentation

For CodeCanyon and Envato assets:

- Lead with monetization and SaaS completeness.
- Show the dashboard, billing, templates, and admin screens.
- Use the social preview image as a primary branded visual.
- Mention Stripe, LemonSqueezy, AI providers, subscriptions, usage credits, and PDF export.
- Keep legal disclaimers clear and responsible.
