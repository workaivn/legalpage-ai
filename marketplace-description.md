# LegalPage AI - AI Legal Page Generator SaaS

LegalPage AI is a premium, production-ready SaaS application for generating legal page drafts for indie makers, SaaS founders, app developers, ecommerce stores, agencies, blogs, marketplaces, and AI startups.

Built with Next.js App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and multi-provider AI support, LegalPage AI helps users create professional legal document drafts such as Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Cookie Policy, Refund Policy, GDPR Compliance Notice, Disclaimer, Acceptable Use Policy, Data Processing Agreement, AI Usage Policy, Affiliate Disclosure, and Copyright Notice.

This is not a simple AI form demo. LegalPage AI includes authentication, a user dashboard, admin dashboard, document management, template library, PDF export, subscriptions, usage tracking, credits, coupons, affiliate referrals, team workspace, white label settings, billing integrations, SEO, documentation, and realistic demo data.
